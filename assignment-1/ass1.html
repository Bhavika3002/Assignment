1.NATURE'S BEAUTY

In nature,everything is perfect .Trees can be contorted,bent in weird ways and they are still beautiful. Plant seeds of happiness,hope,success and love It will all come back to you in abundance.This is the law of nature.


2.TIGER

TIGER is the national animal of India .It is a carnivorous animal.The tiger hunts at night and sleeps during the day. Tigers are strong,powerful and one of the nature's most feared predators.


3.RAINBOW

Rainbow contains a pattern of seven colours- Violet,Indigo,Blue,Green,Yellow,Orange,Red .It is an arc of colour which appears in the sky when sun shines after the rainfall. It is the beautiful phenomena of nature.


4.WATERFALL

Water is the most perfect traveller because when it travels it becomes the path itself.There is a hidden message in every waterfall.It says, if you are flexible,falling will not hurt you .Waterfall cannot be silent,just as wisdom.When they speak , the voice of power speaks.
